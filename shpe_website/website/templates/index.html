Who We Are

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) at UVA is dedicated to promoting engineering and STEM fields within the Hispanic/Latino community. We focus on recruitment, retention, and supporting diversity while encouraging academic, personal, and professional growth among our members.

Through career development programs, workshops, networking events, and social activities, we create opportunities for our members to excel in their academic pursuits and professional careers.

Our Mission

SHPE changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support, and development.

We are brought together by heritage, social responsibility, and a desire to improve equality for all people through the use of science and technology. We value excellence in education, professional pursuits, and leadership.

We obtain excellence through integrity, empowerment, achievement, diversity, and continuous improvement while enhancing pride within our organization and reinforcing our reputation as a vital Hispanic organization.

Juntos Podemos

"Together We Can" - Our signature outreach program that welcomes high school juniors and seniors from across Virginia to explore the University of Virginia alongside current SHPE students.
